The Bottom Line

Warming temperatures and rain will start making the snow surface wet and weak. As you travel, keep an eye out for changing conditions and look for signs of instability such as rollerballs or recent wet loose avalanches. Be ready to dial it back and move to lower-angle slopes once you start seeing these warning signs.
